# Ignite: Welcome to Your Spiritual Journey

Welcome to **Ignite**! This app is designed to help you deepen your faith, connect with your local parish, and build consistent daily spiritual habits in a fun and rewarding way. 

This handbook will guide you through everything you need to know to get the most out of the Ignite experience.

---

## 🌟 The Core Experience: Grace Points & Levels

At the heart of Ignite is a rewarding progression system. As you participate in your faith journey—both inside the app and out in the real world—you earn **Grace Points (XP)**. 

*   **Level Up:** Accumulating Grace Points increases your spiritual Level. 
*   **Daily Streaks:** Logging in and completing daily habits builds your streak. Don't break it!
*   **Badges:** Unlock special achievements for major milestones (like completing a Biblical Journey or a 30-day streak).

---

## 📱 Features & Tools

### 1. The Dashboard
Your home base. Here you will find your current Level, Grace Points, and daily streak. The dashboard also highlights **Announcements** from your parish and **Upcoming Events** you won't want to miss.

### 2. QR Code Event Check-ins
When you attend a church event, youth group, or special mission, look for the Ignite QR code!
*   **How it works:** Tap the **Scan** button in the app and point your camera at the QR code.
*   **Sermon Notes:** To earn your Grace Points, you'll be asked to leave a brief, thoughtful reflection on the event (like a key takeaway or favorite verse).
*   *Note: Our system reviews all reflections to ensure they are genuine, so make sure to write thoughtful notes!*

### 3. Biblical Journeys
Think of this as an interactive map for your faith. 
*   Enroll in courses that guide you through scripture, church history, and theology.
*   Complete nodes along the path—such as reading a passage or taking a quick quiz.
*   Earn **Stars** and massive Grace Points for completing entire courses.

### 4. The Prayer Wall
A safe, supportive space for our community.
*   **Post Requests:** Share what's on your heart. You can choose to post publicly or completely **Anonymously**.
*   **Pray for Others:** Tap the "Pray" button on other people's requests to let them know they are being lifted up in prayer.

### 5. Mentorship & Q&A
Have a tough theological question or need spiritual advice? 
*   Submit questions to church leaders and mentors.
*   You can ask questions anonymously if you prefer privacy. 
*   Get thoughtful, biblically-based answers directly in the app.

### 6. The Leaderboard
A little friendly competition to encourage everyone to stay engaged!
*   **Individual Rank:** See how your Grace Points stack up against the global Ignite community.
*   **Parish Rank:** Work together with your local church to boost your Parish's total score and climb the parish leaderboards.

---

## ⚙️ Getting Started

1.  **Create Your Profile:** Sign up and add your name.
2.  **Join Your Parish:** If your local church uses Ignite, enter their Invite Code or select them from the list to sync your account with their events and leaderboards.
3.  **Complete a Daily Mission:** Start earning Grace Points right away by completing today's featured mission or reading!

We are so excited to have you on this journey. Let's grow together!
